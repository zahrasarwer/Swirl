WHY MECHANICAL ENGINEERING?

Mechanical engineering is where ideas turn into machines.

From engines and supercars to robots and energy systems, this field is about designing, building, and improving the things that move the world.

If you enjoy problem-solving, understanding how things work, and creating real, tangible results — mechanical engineering gives you the power to build, not just imagine.
